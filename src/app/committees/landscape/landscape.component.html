Landscape Committee

The Landscape Committee is an official WST HOA committee.
This year has been especially productive for the Landscape Committee. We have a great team of people who have put in many hours of sweat planting, trimming, weeding and watering.
Seth has been a fantastic committee leader for his first year, and has knowledge of plants and horticulture has been invaluable.
Volunteer
