Events Committee
Leader: Theresa Cutright
The Events Committee is an official WST HOA committee.
The Events Committee is tasked with planning and implementing community events such as holiday parties and other social gatherings. Each year we usually gather around the pool for July 4, Memorial Day and Halloween.
Volunteer
The Events Committee does not meet on any regular basis, but jumps into action when needed. For more information become a WSTAG member and join our WhatsApp Group.
