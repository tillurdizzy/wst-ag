By-Laws
Summary of Major By-law Changes
  • 3.2 Voting: Voting may be done either in person or by online means.
  • 3.5 Proxies: Proxies must be submitted to the Secretary or Assistant Secretary of the Association at least 24 hours before the appointed time of the meeting for which the proxy is intended. No owner shall hold more than two proxies for any given meeting. The Secretary or Assistant Secretary shall certify all proxies before the meeting to ensure their validity and authenticity.
  • 5.8b Written Minutes and Recordings:(i) All Board of Directors meetings, whether held in person or through virtual platforms such as Zoom, shall be recorded to ensure accurate documentation of the proceedings. (ii) For in-person meetings, the Secretary of the Association shall be responsible for taking detailed written Minutes of each meeting, including a summary of discussions, decisions, and any actions taken. (iii) In the case of virtual meetings conducted via platforms like Zoom, the meeting shall be recorded in its entirety to capture all discussions and deliberations.
  • 5.8c Distribution of Meeting Records: (i) Minutes or recorded meeting files shall be made accessible to all homeowners within a reasonable time frame after the meeting, not exceeding seven (7) calendar days. (ii) The Minutes or recordings shall be made available through electronic means, such as email or the Association's official website or other approved website unless a homeowner specifically requests a physical copy.
  • 7.2 Qualifications and Requirements for Officers. To ensure proper representation and understanding of the community's needs, the President and Vice-President must reside full-time on the property. It is essential to maintain a clear distinction between the Board of Directors and the Officers. Therefore, no two persons residing in the same unit or any individuals related by blood, marriage, or other family ties shall serve together on the Board or as Officers, except they may serve together if one person is on the Board, and the other person serves as an Officer.
  • 7.3 Election of Officers. The officers of the Association shall be elected annually by the Board of Directors during the organization meeting of each new Board and shall hold office subject to the continuing approval of the Board. Board members are prohibited from appointing/electing themselves as Officers, ensuring the independence and effectiveness of the Board's decision-making process,and providing oversight and transparency of Officers.
