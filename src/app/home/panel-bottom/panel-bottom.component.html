By-Laws Update
Below is the opening paragraph to the current WST By-laws. As is obvious, they were written by and for the original developer, not the homeowners. They are therefore heavily skewed toward a powerful president rather than a limited and accountable one.
It is a the top priority of WSTAG to update the WST by-laws so homeowners have a greater say in how their investment is managed.
