At this time, it is the top priority of WSTAG to update the by-laws so as to prevent the unscrupulous Boards from remaining in power long past the wishes of the Members. The current Board is unwilling to make these changes, and must therefore be removed by petition before we can more forward.
Petition for a Special Meeting
Please understand! The petition alone does not remove the current board members named in the petition, it only calls for a Special Meeting to vote on the issue. Our by-laws require 1/3 of all Members to sign. Once the requirements are met, the board must hold the meeting. Just as in regular annual meetings, a quorum is required and the vote must be at least 51% of the voting members present to pass.
To Sign the Petition
Contact other members through our WhatsApp Group (click the WhatsApp menu item above for information.)
Voting Proxies at the Special Meeting
Signing the petition is just the first step. You must also show up at the meeting and vote. However, we can use the same tactic that has been so effectively used against us all these years to win back the Board. If you are unable to attend, you can sign your vote over to another member by proxie. Instructions and forms for doing this will be available once the meeting date has been set.
What Will Happen at the Meeting
The first order of business will be to vote on the specific purpose of the meeting to remove the current President and Vice-president. After that passes with at least 51% voting for, a motion will be put forward to extend the Special Meeting to include other business. After that passes with a 2/3's majority, we will carry on with a vote to update the bylaws with the proposed changes.
