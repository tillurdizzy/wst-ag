Why was the WST Advocacy Group formed?
The Westbury Square Townhomes Advocacy Group exists to bring Members together for a common cause and provide a means to make your voice heard. Many of you are sick and tired of the political drama and have simply given up. You have no interest to get involved or participate in the politics of our community. We understand! But...
Now is the time to make a difference!
As many of you know, there have been several efforts in the past to remove Board members or change the bylaws which have all fallen flat. They lacked the organization and communication necessary for a successful campaign for change. We have that now. Through this website and other social platforms we are better prepared to see this through than ever before.
We Need a Change of Management!
  • Our reserve fund is at an all time low.
  • A complete financial audit, required every year under the bylaws, has not been done since 2020.
  • An annual statement showing receipts, expenses and disbursements (as required by the bylaws) as not been prepared since 2020.
  • Monthly financial reports have not been updated since Feb 2022.
  • Minutes from the Quarterly Open Board of Director meetings have not been posted since June 2022.
  • At the beginning of 2022 we were promised a rigourous effort to collect delinquent accounts, and still there have been no significant progress.
  • Many roofs are still in need of replacing.
  • Two of our three water boilers are in need of major repairs.
  • Almost half the the security cameras are out of commission.
  • Rodents and roaches are rampant in certain areas.
  • Laundry machines are constantly out of order and two machines missing all together.
