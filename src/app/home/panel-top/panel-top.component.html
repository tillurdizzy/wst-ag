What is the WST Advocacy Group?
The Westbury Square Townhomes Advocacy Group exists to bring Members together for a common cause and provide a means to make your voice heard. Together we can hold the Board accountable and put an end to the secretive, self-serving and corrupt boards that have been a hallmark of the past decade and more. Many of you are sick and tired of the political drama and have simply given up. You have no interest to get involved or participate in the politics of our community. We understand! But...
Now is the time to make a difference!
As many of you know, there have been several efforts in the past to remove Board members or change the bylaws which have all fallen flat. They lacked the organization and communication necessary for a successful campaign for change. We have that now. Through this website and other social platforms we are better prepared to see this through than ever before.
We can do this!
Our first priority is to update the bylaws to eliminate the loopholes which have allowed unscrupulous Boards to remain in power year after year. Unfortunately, the current Board is opposed to these changes, and must therefore be removed before we can move forward. It's sad that we have to resort to petitions and forced removal, but that is where we find ourselves.
