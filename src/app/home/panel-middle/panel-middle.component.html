About the WST Advocacy Group
The Westbury Square Townhomes Advocacy Group exists to provide homeowners with current, complete and accurate concerning the management, finances and leadership of the HOA. The WST HOA Board of Directors works for, and should be held accountable to the members of the Association (the "Members"). The Westbury Square Townhomes Advocacy Group is dedicated to providing WST homeowners with up-to-date and accurate information concerning the HOA Board of Directors and other community events.
