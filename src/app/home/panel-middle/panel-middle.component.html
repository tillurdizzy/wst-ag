Update to the Bylaws
Anyone opposed to updating our bylaws has their own personal interests in mind, and not what is best for our comminity.
The following changes to the by-laws will eliminate the corruption and allow for a greatest participation of all our Members in the election process.
  • Limit the number of proxies any single owner may hold
  • Separate the Board of Directors from the Officers
  • Require the President and Vice-President to reside on the property
  • Allow secure online voting
Because our bylaws were written in 1978 (before the internet!), there are many instances where the language is vague, outdated and fails to incorporate modern technology. For a full copy of the bylaws with all the proposed changes highlighted, please become a member of WSTAG.
